Finding Articles Help Topics


There are a number of ways to locate articles within the KB.

  1. Click on the "Browse Articles" link. - to view ALL articles by product or by type.
  2. Enter a search word/phrase in the Search box - to quickly search for required article.
  3. Click Advanced link - to view particular aticles by type, product or group.
  4. Approvers or Administrators can use the Admin link from the tool bar and choose "Manage Articles" which provides many options for searching and even exporting the results.
